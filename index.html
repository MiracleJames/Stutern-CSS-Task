The difference between Padding, Margin and Border

  1. Content - The innermost part of the box. Content could be an image, text, heading, or something else.
  2. Padding - The inner space between the content and the border of your box.
  3. Border - The perimeter of the box. Borders can be invisible or they could be a thick colored line like the green one pictured above.
  4. Margin - The outer space (or lack of space) surrounding the box.

The main difference between padding and margin is that padding controls how much breathing room exists within a box, while margin controls how much breathing room or whitespace exists outside of a box. In summary, I would say padding is the space between the content and the border, whereas margin is the space outside the border.
